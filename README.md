# 🧠 Asasanta Nexus — AI Trust Infrastructure on Pharos Blockchain

## 🚀 Overview

Asasanta Nexus is an AI-powered digital trust infrastructure designed to solve identity fraud, low trust in digital services, and the lack of transparent verification systems.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence and blockchain technology to analyze user trust, detect fraud, generate trust proofs, and issue verifiable digital trust certificates secured through the Pharos ecosystem.

---

# ❗ Problem

Millions of people and organizations face challenges with:

- Identity fraud
- Fake digital records
- Lack of reliable trust scoring
- No transparent way to verify digital reputation

Traditional systems rely on centralized databases that can be manipulated or difficult to verify.

---

# 💡 Solution

Asasanta Nexus introduces an intelligent AI Trust Agent that:

- Verifies digital identities
- Calculates AI-based trust scores
- Detects suspicious behavior and fraud risks
- Makes automated trust decisions
- Generates blockchain-ready trust proofs
- Issues publicly verifiable digital trust certificates

# ⚙️ Core Features

## 🪪 Identity Verification Skill

Analyzes identity data and provides:

- Verification status
- Confidence score
- Risk level

---

## 📊 Trust Score AI

Evaluates user reliability based on:

- Identity status
- Transaction history
- Account activity

Produces a trust score from 0–100.

---

## 🚨 Fraud Detection AI

Detects high-risk activities such as:

- Suspicious login attempts
- New device risks
- Unusual transactions

---

## 🤖 AI Trust Agent

Combines all AI skills and generates a final decision:

- Approved
- Review Required
- Rejected

---

## 🔐 Pharos Trust Proof Layer

Creates immutable trust records containing:

- Proof ID
- AI trust data
- Verification metadata

---

## ⛓️ Blockchain Certificate Layer

Generates digital trust certificates with:

- Certificate ID
- Trust score
- AI decision
- Blockchain transaction reference
